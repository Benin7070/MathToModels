# Journey to Strong AI

Welcome to my repository! This project serves as a comprehensive log of my progress in mastering Artificial Intelligence and Large Language Models (LLMs). 

The journey is structured progressively from the very basics to advanced topics, spanning from **Stage 0** to **Stage 30**.

## Progression Roadmap
- **Stage 0**: Fundamentals & Prerequisites
- **Stage 1 to Stage 10**: Basics of Machine Learning and Neural Networks
- **Stage 11 to Stage 20**: Deep Learning, NLP, and Core LLM Concepts
- **Stage 21 to Stage 30**: Advanced AI, Multi-modal models, AI Agents, and State-of-the-Art Research

Follow along as I navigate through these stages to build a robust understanding of AI!
